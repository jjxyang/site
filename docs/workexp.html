Highguard (2026)

An online PVP first-person raid shooter built in Unreal Engine 5 and shipped on PC, Playstation 5, and Xbox Series X/S.

I helped ship Highguard in 2022 with a core team of 20 engineers. I built important game systems, such as an item interaction system that deduced player intent and resolved multiple potential actions across input, building, looting, and weapon systems. Moreover, I owned all of our audio integration and dynamic audio systems. My contributions also involved VFX, audio optimization, voice chat integration, settings, and destruction systems.

Overwatch

An online PVP first-person team-based hero shooter built in a proprietary ECS engine and shipped on PC, Xbox, Playstation, and Switch.

I helped ship Overwatch 2 in 2022 with a core team of 60+ engineers and supported a wide variety of gameplay, cosmetic, and UI/UX development for many Overwatch live service patches, often participating in rapid prototyping and playtesting to realize heroes like Venture and game modes like “Starwatch: Galactic Rescue.” On the daily, I interfaced with strike teams of animators, artists, and designers of all stripes to parse feedback, ideate solutions, and implement new features. I even rallied for team-wide education on non-binary issues to ensure we created our first non-binary hero, Venture, with care.

Starwatch: Galactic Rescue

A limited-time game mode developed for an Overwatch 2 live service patch, released in 2023. Overwatch's first PVP/PVE hybrid event, where opposing teams face off to either capture or defend four points on the map. Features map hazards and teammate-revival mechanics.

In collaboration with a handful of artists and designers, I participated in the rapid prototyping process, updated the engine to support new gameplay elements, assisted with debugging, and provided other ad hoc support.
